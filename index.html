About

Elections are held to ensure democracy in countries. There are candidates who will represent the country, who will take important steps for the future of the country, and voters who will vote for these candidates. The votes of everyone who voted in these elections play an important role in this direction. Voting should be reliable and transparent, and privacy should be ensured. Afterwards, this process should not take long for a healthy and efficient result. Waiting for a long time brings to mind the idea of manipulating the votes. These manipulation events in paper selections also occur quite frequently. In order to eliminate such problems, it is useful to use votes in online systems. The e-voting system will facilitate the election process. However, this system should not have any open, privacy and security concerns. Blockchain technology is used to eliminate these concerns. In this way, the e-voting system is developed. Blockchain is a mechanism that will best ensure security, privacy and data integrity for voters. It has many features. The chains in the blockchain cannot be changed and since all the information contained in it is recorded in many places, its accuracy is confirmed. This would be the solution to the over-central problem. Changing the information in a block is not possible because it is noticed by other blocks. Blockchain increases information security as it keeps information in all its blocks. All chains are interconnected. Therefore, all information is kept in the last node. It is sufficient to search only the last node for results. This will greatly reduce the time to wait for the election result.
